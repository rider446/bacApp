Calculate your BAC

Drinking and driving is a major problem in our society which takes countless lives each year. Use this calculator to determine your BAC. Buzzed driving is drunk driving.

Start Here!

Knowing your BAC can help you make a informed decision!

I developed this web application to help people make a better informed decision using BAC, which stands for Blood Alcohol Content. The higher the level the more alcohol is in your system. Most times people do not realize how drunk they are when they get behind the wheel. The main purpose of this project is to help people avoid getting behind the wheel even when buzzed.
